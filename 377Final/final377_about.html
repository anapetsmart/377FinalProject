How to use the site:

The home page of the website allows you to filter through the dog breeds and look at different pictures. People will be able to filter alphabetically, and reverse alphabetically. The home page will display a picture of the breed and some short facts. The Find a Breed page will allow people to search for specific breeds and provide a image carousel of different pictures of the breed with some more information listed. The Catalog page is just an image carousel of all the different breeds that users can arrow through.
